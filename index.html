Work History

AVP Technology

During 2019, I interned at AVP Technology, a company based in Fremont, Califronia that specializes in refurbishing wafer film equipments. From photolithography tools to etching/cleaning tools, I have helped their engineers on site assemble/disassemble these very important(and expensive) machines to improve their design and renew parts. I later interned in GIS solutions, a similar company based in Suzhou, China which focuses on lithographic machines as an extension from this experience.
